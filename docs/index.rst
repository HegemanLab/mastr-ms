Mastr-MS
========

Mastr-MS is a web-based tool for experimental design, sample metadata
configuration, and sample data acquisition.

Metabolomics Australia Sample Tracking Repository
-------------------------------------------------

Mastr-MS is a collaborative project undertaken by the Australian
Bioinformatics Facility (ABF), located at the `Centre for Comparative
Genomics`_, for Metabolomics Australia (MA).

.. _Centre for Comparative Genomics: http://ccg.murdoch.edu.au/

Mastr-MS is comprised of a number of modules:

**User Management Module**

  A secure online user management module for the Metabolomics
  Australia Data and Sample Management System.
    
**Sample Management Module**

  An online sample management module allowing users to submit samples,
  track samples through experimental workflows and receive results.

**Data Management Module**

  Provision of secure, online metabolomics repository to capture all
  relevant MA experimental workflows. It includes storage of raw data
  files as well as all relevant meta data associated with an
  experiment.
